# Product Definition

**Project Name:** Immediate Tsunami and Storm Surge Population Impact Modeling

## Project Description
A geospatial data pipeline that predicts building-level flood damage from hurricanes and tsunamis to support American Red Cross shelter and casework planning.

## Problem Statement
The Red Cross lacks a rapid, data-driven way to estimate population impacts in the initial hours of a disaster for mass care planning. Operations currently rely heavily on incomplete ad-hoc local reporting or delayed federal assessments.

## Target Users
Red Cross disaster response staff and operational planners with limited technical skills who need rapid, actionable impact analytics.

## Key Goals
1. Provide building-level damage predictability within hours of a major event.
2. Integrate output seamlessly into existing Excel-based shelter and response workflows.
3. Build a scalable, modular geospatial data pipeline based on open standards (PDDL, Python, cloud storage).
